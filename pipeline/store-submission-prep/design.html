SnowRaven Mini — Store Assets

The visual direction for the Chrome Web Store and Firefox Add-ons listings, designed inside the existing SnowRaven brand (quiet utility, restrained green, the raven mark). Two things get produced: the 440×280 Chrome promo tile, and a 1280×800 screenshot template that frames the real popup on a calm branded canvas. Everything below renders at its true pixel size.

1 · Chrome small promo tile

Required by the Chrome Web Store. 440×280, PNG. Shows the icon, the wordmark, a one-line tagline, and a small peek of the real weather output so the tile says what the tool does, not just what it is called.

Design notes

Brand: existing SnowRaven system, unchanged. Green #2D8653, Inter, the raven mark. Promo tile: light canvas so the green icon and the output peek carry the color, not a flat green block. Screenshots: real popup, real output bytes, framed on a calm canvas. Chrome requires screenshots show the actual UI. Dark variants: shots 1 and 2 also shipped in dark, since the extension follows the OS theme. Marquee tile (1400×560): out of scope, optional, featured-placement only. Firefox: reuses the same screenshots; AMO needs no promo tile.
